Notes on this mockup

  • Carousel scorecards, not Tinder swipe. Horizontal scroll between candidates, snap-to-card. No left/right rejection mechanic — this is browsing, not sorting.
  • Party-colored cards. Republican = red theme, Democrat = blue, Independent = purple. The hero strip and accent buttons take the party color so the card identity is obvious at a glance.
  • Federal midterms only. House, Senate, and Governor. No state legislature, no municipal, no general elections in this iteration.
  • Track-record annotations are the wedge vs. ISideWith. We surface "Voted YES on H.R.X" or "Top donor: Industry Y contradicts stance" — the gap between what they say and what they do.
  • Free-text matcher uses Anthropic Haiku to interpret the user's prose and rank candidates.
  • All names and data are illustrative. Real data comes from Ballotpedia, OpenSecrets, ProPublica, and FEC.
